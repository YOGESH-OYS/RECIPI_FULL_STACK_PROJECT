Chiles Rellenos

Instructions: Roast the poblano peppers until the skins are blackened. Peel off the skin, remove the seeds, and set them aside. Prepare the stuffing: Sauté ground beef with onions, garlic, and tomatoes, or simply use cheese.

Stuff the peppers with the filling and dip them in egg batter before frying. Serve with a simple tomato sauce and garnish with cilantro.

Sopes

Instructions:
Shape small portions of masa into thick disks, then cook them on a hot griddle. Pinch the edges of the masa to form a small rim around the edge to hold toppings.

Spread a layer of refried beans over each sope, then top with lettuce, cheese, sour cream, salsa, and cilantro. Serve with a squeeze of lime.
